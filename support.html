Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Block-GoGoGo?

Block-GoGoGo is a match-3 puzzle game. Tap a block to select it, then tap an adjacent block to swap their positions. Create matches of 3 or more identical blocks to clear them and earn points.

Why can't I swap certain blocks?

You can only swap adjacent blocks (horizontally or vertically). If the swap doesn't create a match of 3 or more blocks, the blocks will automatically swap back to their original positions.

How do I earn more moves?

You start with 30 moves. When you level up by reaching certain score thresholds, you'll receive additional moves. Plan your moves carefully to maximize your score!

The game crashes or freezes. What should I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Close the app completely and restart it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version of the game installed
  • Check that your device has enough available storage space

How do I turn off sound or music?

You can adjust sound and music settings in the game's Settings menu. Tap the settings icon in the game to access audio controls.

Can I play offline?

Yes! Block-GoGoGo can be played without an internet connection. However, some features like advertisements may require an internet connection.

How do I reset my progress?

To reset your progress, go to the Settings menu and look for the "Reset Progress" or "Restart Game" option. Please note that this action cannot be undone.
